Hi, I'm Ryan Snyder.

I am a web enthusiast and food writer who lives in Portland, Oregon.

I write about charcuterie on Meat Club, restaurant recommendations for travelers on Noms.in, and wine on Winegeeks.

I founded and maintain the recipe platform Foodgeeks, the wine review website Winegeeks, and the food festivals directory In Praise of Food.

I serve as an advisor to Salemarked and KBlaster.

Formerly, I worked for Mozilla and Simple, was a co-founder of the location-based service Shizzow, and was the founder of the Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer training platform Cram Session.

My non-food writing can be found on my blog, and Scribd.

You may find me on Twitter, Facebook, Flickr, and LinkedIn.
